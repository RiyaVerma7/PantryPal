🥗🍞 Weekly Meal Planner 🥑🍛

Welcome to your 7-day healthy meal planner! 🌿 This planner helps you save time, eat better, and bring variety to your weekly routine. Start your journey towards healthier eating, one meal at a time! ✨

🗓️ Monday 🥯
🥣 Breakfast

Classic Oatmeal

With berries & nuts.

~380 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Cook 1/2 cup oats in 1 cup milk or water.
  2. Top with mixed berries and a handful of nuts.
  3. Add honey or maple syrup for sweetness.
🥗 Lunch

Quinoa Salad

With chickpeas & veggies.

~450 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Cook 1 cup quinoa as per instructions.
  2. Add cooked chickpeas, diced cucumber, tomato, and bell pepper.
  3. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
🍲 Dinner

Lentil Curry

Served with rice.

~500 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add lentils, tomatoes, and spices (cumin, turmeric, chili powder).
  3. Simmer until lentils are soft and serve with cooked rice.
🗓️ Tuesday 🌮
🥣 Breakfast

Avocado Toast

On whole-grain bread.

~320 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Toast 2 slices of whole-grain bread.
  2. Mash 1 avocado and spread on toast.
  3. Top with salt, pepper, and cherry tomatoes.
🥗 Lunch

Veggie Wrap

Grilled veggies & hummus.

~400 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Grill sliced zucchini, bell peppers, and carrots.
  2. Spread hummus on a whole wheat wrap.
  3. Fill with veggies, roll, and slice.
🍲 Dinner

Stuffed Peppers

Filled with quinoa.

~480 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Cook 1 cup quinoa and mix with black beans and corn.
  2. Cut bell peppers in half and remove seeds.
  3. Stuff peppers with mixture, bake 25 min at 180°C.
🗓️ Wednesday 🥘
🥣 Breakfast

Smoothie Bowl

Blended fruits & granola.

~350 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Blend 1 banana, 1/2 cup berries, and 1/2 cup milk.
  2. Pour into bowl, top with granola and sliced fruits.
  3. Sprinkle chia seeds if desired.
🥗 Lunch

Chickpea Salad

With cucumber & tomato.

~420 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Mix chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, onion.
  2. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Top with parsley or coriander.
🍲 Dinner

Veggie Stir Fry

With tofu & soy sauce.

~470 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Sauté vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers) in oil.
  2. Add tofu cubes and stir fry 5-7 mins.
  3. Season with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.
🗓️ Thursday 🍎
🥣 Breakfast

Banana Pancakes

With maple syrup.

~400 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Mash 1 banana, mix with 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup milk, 1 tsp baking powder.
  2. Cook on non-stick pan 2-3 mins per side.
  3. Serve with maple syrup and berries.
🥗 Lunch

Caprese Sandwich

Mozzarella, tomato & basil.

~390 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Layer mozzarella slices, tomato slices, and basil leaves on whole-grain bread.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
  3. Serve immediately.
🍲 Dinner

Vegetable Soup

With lentils & spices.

~350 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and carrots.
  2. Add lentils, diced vegetables, vegetable broth, and spices.
  3. Simmer until vegetables are soft. Serve hot.
🗓️ Friday 🍕
🥣 Breakfast

Granola Parfait

With yogurt & fruit.

~330 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Layer yogurt, granola, and fresh fruit in a glass.
  2. Repeat layers and top with a drizzle of honey.
  3. Serve immediately.
🥗 Lunch

Falafel Bowl

With hummus & veggies.

~480 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Bake or fry falafel balls.
  2. Serve over greens, cucumber, tomato, and hummus.
  3. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
🍲 Dinner

Veggie Pasta

With tomato basil sauce.

~520 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Cook pasta according to instructions.
  2. Sauté vegetables in olive oil, add tomato sauce and seasonings.
  3. Toss pasta with sauce and serve.
🗓️ Saturday 🥑
🥣 Breakfast

Chia Pudding

With almond milk & fruits.

~300 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Mix 3 tbsp chia seeds with 1 cup almond milk.
  2. Refrigerate overnight.
  3. Top with berries and nuts before serving.
🥗 Lunch

Grilled Veg Sandwich

With cheese & herbs.

~410 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Grill sliced veggies (bell pepper, zucchini, onion).
  2. Layer on bread with cheese and herbs.
  3. Toast until cheese melts.
🍲 Dinner

Paneer Curry

Served with roti.

~500 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices, then paneer cubes.
  3. Cook for 10 min and serve with roti.
🗓️ Sunday 🥞
🥣 Breakfast

French Toast

With cinnamon & syrup.

~370 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Dip bread slices in beaten eggs with cinnamon.
  2. Cook on skillet 2-3 mins per side.
  3. Serve with syrup and berries.
🥗 Lunch

Veggie Buddha Bowl

With quinoa, avocado & chickpeas.

~450 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Cook quinoa and place in bowl.
  2. Add chickpeas, roasted veggies, and avocado slices.
  3. Top with tahini dressing.
🍲 Dinner

Veg Pizza

Whole wheat base & cheese.

~550 kcal

Recipe:

  1. Spread tomato sauce on whole wheat pizza base.
  2. Add vegetables and sprinkle cheese on top.
  3. Bake at 200°C for 12-15 mins and serve hot.
